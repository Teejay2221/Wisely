About Wisely

Helping students and individuals build smarter financial habits through expense tracking, budgeting, and financial insights.

🎯 Our Mission

Wisely empowers users to take control of their finances by providing simple tools for tracking expenses, understanding spending patterns, and making informed financial decisions.

📚 Capstone Purpose

This project was developed as a capstone application to demonstrate practical solutions for personal financial management using modern web technologies.

🚀 Why Choose Wisely?

Monitor expenses, analyze spending behavior, and gain financial insights within a clean and user-friendly platform.
